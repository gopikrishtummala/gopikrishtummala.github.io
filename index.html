Gopi Krishna Tummala

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University. I am co-advised by Dr. Prasun Sinha and Dr. Rajiv Ramnath. The primary focus of my research is solving emerging issues in V2V, V2I communications, and sensing applications. I have worked with Honda, Microsoft Research (internship), Qualcomm Autonomous Research (internship) on various vehicular projects.

Before joining Ohio State, I obtained a B.Tech in Electrical Engineering, from the Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Madras. For my final thesis at IIT Madras, I have worked with Prof. K. Giridhar.
