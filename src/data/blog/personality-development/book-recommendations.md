A curated collection of books that have shaped my thinking on personal development, philosophy, psychology, and life. These are all from my personal library—books I've read, re-read, or am currently exploring.

## Personal Development & Productivity

- **Atomic Habits** by James Clear
- **The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People** by Stephen R. Covey
- **Tools of Titans** by Tim Ferriss
- **The 5AM Club** by Robin Sharma
- **Can't Hurt Me** by David Goggins
- **Never Split the Difference** by Chris Voss
- **How to Lead When You're Not in Charge** by Clay Scroggins

## Psychology & Mindset

- **The Psychology of Money** by Morgan Housel
- **Emotional Intelligence** by Daniel Goleman
- **The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck** by Mark Manson
- **Think Like a Monk** by Jay Shetty
- **Declutter Your Mind** by S.J. Scott and Barrie Davenport
- **Mind Mapping** by Kam Knight

## Philosophy & Spirituality

- **Krishnamurti: Reflections on the Self** by Jiddu Krishnamurti
- **Mind and Its Control** by Swami Budhananda
- **Karma: A Yogi's Guide to Crafting Your Destiny** by Sadhguru
- **Inner Engineering: A Yogi's Guide to Joy** by Jaggi Vasudev (Sadhguru)
- **The Book of Joy** by Dalai Lama and Desmond Tutu
- **Metahuman: Unleashing Your Infinite Potential** by Deepak Chopra
- **The Power of Letting Go** by John Purkiss

## Happiness & Well-being

- **Solve for Happy** by Mo Gawdat
- **Ikigai: The Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life** by Héctor García and Francesc Miralles
- **The Anatomy of Loneliness** by Teal Swan
- **Life Force** by Tony Robbins, Peter H. Diamandis, and Robert Hariri

## Business & Technology

- **The Four: The Hidden DNA of Amazon, Apple, Facebook, and Google** by Scott Galloway

## Memoirs & Life Stories

- **I'm Glad My Mom Died** by Jennette McCurdy
- **Born a Crime** by Trevor Noah
- **The Last Black Unicorn** by Tiffany Haddish
- **I Can't Make This Up** by Kevin Hart
- **What I Talk about When I Talk about Running** by Haruki Murakami

## Relationships & Communication

- **Men Are from Mars, Women Are from Venus** by John Gray

## Organization & Lifestyle

- **The Life-Changing Magic of Tidying Up** by Marie Kondo

## Fiction & Classics

- **Harry Potter Series** by J.K. Rowling
  - Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone
  - Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets
  - Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban
  - Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire
  - Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix
- **Sherlock Holmes: The Definitive Collection** by Arthur Conan Doyle
- **The Immortals of Meluha - The Shiva Trilogy** by Amish Tripathi
